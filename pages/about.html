About us:


We are a wholesale importer of high-quality handcrafted felt, textile and ceramics produced by Art Group Tumar, a felt atelier based in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan.


Ordering information:


Orders will be shipped as soon as possible. As these products are beautifully and individually hand-crafted, however, inventories are limited and items may need to be produced to order. Consequently, not all orders can be filled immediately. Please allow up to two months for orders to be completed and shipped from Kyrgyzstan.

Central Asia Imports will pay for all shipping and import fees from Kyrgyzstan.

Customer pays for all shipping and handling fees from Central Asia Imports to final destination in the United States.

Return policy:
Items can be returned to Central Asia Imports within 14 days of receipt. Returned item must be in original condition, with all tags on item, and in original packaging.

Buyer will pay return shipping fees.

Upon inspection, if returned item meets above requirements, a refund will be given to buyer according to method of purchase.
